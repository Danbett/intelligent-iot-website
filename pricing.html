Frequently Asked Questions

How is hardware pricing handled?+

Hardware (GPS trackers, sensors, gateways) is priced separately from the platform subscription. We source best-in-class hardware at competitive rates and can include it in an all-in monthly fee or bill it one-time. We're hardware-agnostic — if you have existing devices, we can integrate them.

Is there a minimum contract period?+

We offer monthly, annual, and multi-year agreements. Annual plans come with a significant discount. Enterprise clients typically operate on 2–3 year agreements with performance-based SLAs.

What connectivity does the platform require?+

Our platform works on 2G, 3G, 4G, and satellite connectivity. Devices have onboard storage for offline data buffering — so gaps in coverage don't mean gaps in data. We've successfully deployed in remote areas across Uganda, Rwanda, and Northern Kenya — and in comparable low-connectivity environments worldwide.

Can I try the platform before committing?+

Yes — request a demo for instant access to our live environment pre-loaded with real scenarios. For enterprise clients, we also offer a 30-day pilot with actual hardware at a reduced commitment.
