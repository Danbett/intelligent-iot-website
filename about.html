Our Story

Built in Africa. Deployed Worldwide.

We are a team of engineers, data scientists, and IoT specialists on a mission to deliver world-class connected technology to enterprises of every scale, in every market.

Who We Are

Enterprise IoT for the World's Toughest Environments

Intelligent IoT was founded with a single conviction: every enterprise on earth deserves world-class connected technology — designed for their actual infrastructure, climate, and operational realities.

We started where the challenges are most acute — fleets operating without real data, farms wasting water without sensors, factories running to failure with no visibility. We built a platform robust enough for the world's most demanding conditions.

Headquartered in Nairobi, Kenya and serving clients globally — our platform is proven across diverse network conditions, power environments, and operational contexts that mirror the real world, not a data centre.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Reliability First

Our systems operate in remote fields, moving vehicles, and critical healthcare settings. Reliability isn't a feature — it's the baseline.

Built for Reality

We design for low-bandwidth environments, intermittent power, and diverse field conditions — not for ideal lab settings or Silicon Valley data centres.

Partner Mindset

We don't sell boxes. We build long-term relationships — measuring success by the business outcomes our clients achieve.

Data Transparency

Your data stays yours. We provide full data portability, clear audit trails, and no vendor lock-in on the data we help you generate.
