Fleet Telematics
Nairobi, Kenya

Nairobi Logistics Group — Fleet Intelligence Platform

A 120-vehicle last-mile delivery operation was experiencing 28% fuel overconsumption and no visibility into driver behaviour. We deployed real-time GPS tracking, CAN bus fuel monitoring, driver behaviour scoring, and predictive maintenance alerts across the entire fleet. The operations manager could see every vehicle live on one screen.

Challenge: No real-time visibility, fuel theft, and reactive (not predictive) maintenance leading to unexpected breakdowns.
Solution: Teltonika FMB140 trackers with CAN bus integration, cloud-hosted fleet dashboard, automated fuel anomaly detection, and weekly driver performance reports.

Healthcare Cold Chain
Uganda & Kenya

East Africa Pharma — Vaccine Cold Chain Monitor

A pharmaceutical distributor supplying vaccines across 200 rural and urban clinics in Uganda and Kenya needed end-to-end cold chain assurance. We deployed temperature and humidity sensors at every storage point — warehouses, delivery vehicles, and clinic fridges — with automated SMS alerts when thresholds were breached.

Challenge: Manual temperature logging, delayed excursion detection, and regulatory compliance failures costing stock and certification.
Solution: Wireless Bluetooth sensors + 4G gateways, real-time cloud dashboard, automated SMS/WhatsApp alerts, and WHO-compliant temperature reports.

Agriculture IoT
Kericho, Kenya

Kericho Tea Estates — Precision Agriculture Network

A 2,400-acre tea plantation in the Kericho Highlands was over-irrigating based on calendar schedules rather than actual soil conditions. We deployed a network of soil moisture, temperature, and nutrient sensors alongside automated weather stations. Irrigation was then triggered only when sensor data indicated genuine crop need.

Challenge: Excessive water usage, inconsistent crop quality, and agronomists making decisions without real-time field data.
Solution: 48 soil sensor nodes, 6 weather stations, automated irrigation control, and a precision farming dashboard with crop health scoring.

Energy & Utilities
Western Kenya

SolarGrid Kenya — Rural Mini-Grid Management

A rural electrification programme powering 15 off-grid communities in Western Kenya needed remote monitoring of solar panels, battery banks, and distribution lines — without expensive on-site technicians at each site. We built a centralised energy management platform with predictive fault detection.

Challenge: Reactive maintenance, 4–6 hour outage response times, and no visibility into energy generation vs. consumption patterns.
Solution: IoT energy meters, solar inverter integration, cloud SCADA dashboard, predictive fault alerts, and automated generator switchover.

Smart Buildings
Kampala, Uganda

Kampala Business Park — Smart Facility Management

An 8-floor commercial complex in Kampala required integration of access control, HVAC monitoring, water tank level management, and energy consumption tracking into a single operations dashboard. Previously managed by 6 separate systems and paper logs.

Challenge: Disconnected building systems, high water waste from unmonitored tank overflows, and inability to track energy usage per floor.
Solution: Unified BMS platform integrating 120 sensors — RFID access, HVAC sensors, water level floats, energy sub-meters, and a single web dashboard for building management.

Water & Environment
Northern Uganda

USAID Water Programme — Borehole Network Monitor

A humanitarian water programme managing 50 boreholes across 12 districts in Northern Uganda needed real-time pump status, water flow rates, and fault detection — without dedicated technicians at each site. Manual inspection was happening once a month, leaving communities without water for weeks when pumps failed silently.

Challenge: Silent pump failures going undetected for days or weeks, no water flow data, and high maintenance costs from reactive site visits.
Solution: Solar-powered IoT nodes on each borehole with flow sensors, pump current monitoring, GPS location, and 2G/satellite connectivity feeding a centralised water management dashboard.
