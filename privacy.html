Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 January 2025

1. Introduction

Intelligent IoT ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you visit our website intelligenceiot.com or use our platform services.

By using our website, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Contact information: Name, email address, phone number, company name — collected when you submit a contact form, request a demo, or subscribe to our newsletter.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, time on site, browser type, IP address, and referral source — collected automatically via cookies and analytics tools.
  • Platform data: IoT sensor readings, device telemetry, and operational data collected and processed on behalf of our enterprise clients under separate data processing agreements.
  • Communications: Messages, enquiries, and feedback you send to us.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and provide customer support
  • Process demo requests and route you to the correct platform
  • Send newsletters and product updates (only with your consent)
  • Improve our website and platform services
  • Analyse website usage through aggregated, anonymised analytics
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. These include:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function correctly (e.g., session management, theme preference).
  • Analytics cookies: Google Analytics (if enabled) to understand site usage. These are anonymised and do not identify individuals.
  • Preference cookies: Remembering your dark/light mode and cookie consent choice.

You may decline non-essential cookies using the cookie banner displayed on your first visit. You can also manage cookies through your browser settings.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Service providers: EmailJS (contact form delivery), Google Analytics (usage analytics), Cloudflare (CDN and security). Each bound by data processing agreements.
  • Legal authorities: Where required by law or to protect our legal rights.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our platform uses TLS encryption for all data in transit and AES-256 encryption for data at rest.

7. Data Retention

We retain contact and enquiry data for up to 3 years unless you request deletion earlier. Website analytics data is retained for 26 months. Platform client data retention is governed by individual service agreements.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time
  • Object to processing of your personal data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@intelligenceiot.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites (e.g., the demo platform). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
